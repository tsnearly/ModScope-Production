Privacy by design

Your data stays
on Reddit.

ModScope is built entirely on Reddit's Devvit platform. There are no external servers, no third-party databases, and no data leaving Reddit's infrastructure.

Zero external data collection.

Every byte of analytics data ModScope generates is stored in Reddit's own native Redis layer — scoped to your subreddit, controlled by Reddit's infrastructure, and never touched by SeeTigerLearn servers (because there are none). No sign-up required. No API keys. No tracking.

How it works

Up and running in
three steps.

01
Install ModScope from the Devvit app directory
One click from the Reddit Developer Platform. No configuration required to get started — ModScope works out of the box with sensible defaults.
02
Set your schedule and tune the algorithm
Choose how often you want ModScope to scan your community. Pick a preset that matches your subreddit's content type, or dial in custom engagement weights if you want full control.
03
Open your Report tab and start learning
After your first scan completes, you have a full analytics dashboard — top posts ranked by genuine engagement, community stats, prime posting times, and a word cloud of your community's most-used language. Every scan after that adds to your historical trend data.
